Honest take from the data

Why I ranked these the way I did
  • The Clearwater and Aquarius by the Park are the best fit to your stated criteria: under 8 min to Bedok Reservoir MRT, town gas, helper's rooms in most 3BR, and 3BR rents that actually have units listed under S$6k.
  • Costa Del Sol is the dark horse. It's on the Thomson-East Coast Line (your brown line) AND walking distance to Tanah Merah (EWL/green) — a real two-line advantage for your office commute. The catch is rent: most 3BR listings start at S$5.5k+.
  • Waterfront Waves / Key / Isle / Gold all sit ~10–12 min from the MRT — they fail a strict 8-min walk reading. They're worth viewing only if a specific unit ticks every other box.
  • Aadya's commute = school bus, full stop. No MRT-then-bus transfers for an 11-year-old. UWCSEA East's bus catchment covers all East-side estates on this list, but routes change yearly — email uwcseaeast@uwcsea.edu.sg with your shortlist to confirm pickup before signing anywhere.
  • What I'd skip: Pasir Ris / Simei don't help — they save 5–10 min for Aadya (which the school bus already handles) but add 10–15 min to your office commute and don't reduce rent meaningfully.

Aadya's commute: UWC East, 1 Tampines St 73

Rule: school bus or single direct bus only. No MRT+bus transfers for an 11-year-old. 40 min door-to-door is the ceiling.

UWC East is not within walking distance of any MRT, so the school bus is the realistic answer for every condo on this list. Pickup is at or near your condo gate, supervised, ~20–32 min door-to-door depending on route.

For the Bedok Reservoir cluster (Clearwater, Aquarius, Baywater, Waterfront *) the school bus is typically 25–32 min. For Costa Del Sol (Bayshore), ~22–28 min. For Tampines condos (Tropica, Tropical Spring), it's 10–18 min and a direct public bus (e.g. 34, 69, 293) is also viable.

Action item (do this before signing anything): email uwcseaeast@uwcsea.edu.sg with your shortlist and ask which condos are on existing bus routes for next academic year. This single answer should drive your final pick.
